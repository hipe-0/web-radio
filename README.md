# web-radio
A practical standalone web radio with 200+ channels, hours of music with (usually almost) no ads, working on ESP32 and connecting to your Wifi box in one push on the WPS button.

# Features
* ESP32 driven
* Connects to internet radio with Wifi antenna from ESP32
* WPS connexion with one click on most wifi box
* 200+ channels are preset
* easy selection of channel with rotary encoder equipped with 2x 10K pullup resistors, press to select
* Display 240x240 LCD screen 3.3v RGB 1.54"x1.54"
* Memorize the last channel
* If a channel produces a rebooting, long press on encoder while rebooting will move to channel+1
* 3D printed enclosure
* 'Audio kit' DAC PT8211 from PRJC I2S 16 bit 3.3v
* +/- volume button
* switch
* Li/ion battery with 5v converter
* 3mm Audio jack for audio output, works with either phone or line-out
